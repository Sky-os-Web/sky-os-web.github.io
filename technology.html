Technology

Real foundations, explained simply.

Sky OS starts at the boot sector and builds upward. The technical details are available, but this page keeps the story readable.

Boot path

Custom BIOS boot sector, stage‑2 loader, LBA disk reads, protected‑mode handoff, and BootInfo v2.

Kernel

32‑bit protected‑mode kernel with interrupts, timer, keyboard, mouse stubs, serial logging, and panic diagnostics.

SkyFS

SkyFS V2 includes typed files, directory-style paths, allocation bitmap, safer writes, and recovery journal records.

Desktop

Launcher, dock, desktop icons, system cards, file panels, mouse cursor, and framebuffer/VGA fallback paths.

Apps

SKYEX app runner, PIDs, process listing, handle layer, and early compatibility inspection for foreign executables.

Hardware direction

CPUID, ACPI, PCI scanning, APIC planning, and future x64, ARM64, RISC‑V, and display backends.
